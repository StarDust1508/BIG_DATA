Big data is more than just a buzzword.
It is a real change in how we store and process information.
Big data systems use distributed storage and distributed computation.
Apache Hadoop was a pioneer in this field.
Hadoop introduced HDFS for storage and MapReduce for computation.
Today Apache Spark is the most popular framework for big data.
Spark improved on Hadoop by keeping data in memory between steps.
Big data is everywhere from banking to medicine to law.
Data privacy and data quality remain hard problems.
Even with big data, the basics still matter most.
